I’m a geographer with specialist interests and experience in; geoinformatics, GIScience, geocomputation and the analysis, management and visualisation of geospatial data.

Based in Worcestershire and occasionally Amsterdam, I’m particularly interested in; urban morphology, vernacular geography, volunteered geographic information (VGI), spatial simulation, qualitative GIS and ambient geographic information (AGI). I’m an advocate of open standards and open source software such as QGIS, PostGIS and RStudio.
